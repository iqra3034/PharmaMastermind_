About PharmaMastermind

PharmaMastermind is an advanced pharmacy management plus decision support system to transform traditional pharmacy operations into a smarter, more efficient process. The system provides a role-based interface for Owner,Admin Employees, and Customers ensuring tailored features and smooth workflow management for each user. Core functionalities include inventory management, billing, order processing, and customer handling, along with integrated business intelligence tools. These tools provide expiry alerts, profit and sales trend analysis, seasonal demand forecasting, and smart restock recommendations, enabling pharmacy owners to make data-driven decisions that enhance profitability and reduce operational risks. By combining ease of use, automation, and analytical insights, PharmaMastermind delivers a complete solution for pharmacies seeking efficiency, accuracy, and sustainable growth in a competitive market.
